Taylor Swift has released a total of ten albums from 2006 to 2022: Taylor Swift, Fearless, Mine, Red, 1989, Reputation, Lover, folklore, evermore and Midnights. Her albums have consistently sold in millions of album-equivalent units in the United States, with Fearless and 1989 being particularly notable for their high sales figures, each surpassing 6 million units sold in the US. The singles from these albums have achieved significant chart success on the Billboard Hot 100. Notably, many singles have reached the top 10, with several reaching the number 1 spot, such as We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together, Shake It Off, Blank Space, Bad Blood, Look What You Made Me Do, and Willow.
